On Thoughts From Places

By Sebastian | September 14, 2024

“I write because I don't know what I think until I read what I say.” Flannery O'Connor

You know when someone brings up a hilarious past memory and you have no idea what they’re talking about? I swear, sometimes I feel like I’ve got early-onset dementia because I’m the only person that has no recollection of these friend-group-defining moments.

I think it’s worse because I generally wander through life without keeping track of the things I’ve done. I always avoided taking pictures of both myself and the things that I’m doing because I never had a positive image of my body. I tried keeping a journal, but never wanted to write in it because I was worried that someone would end up reading it someday.

I’ve decided to start Thoughts From Places as a journal of sorts, I guess. I’m going to try writing about hobbies, projects, and capital-T Things that I’m doing. The name isn’t original — to be clear, I stole the title from vlogbrothers’ identically named playlist “Thoughts from Places”. It’s going to hopefully be a journey of self-discovery, and a place to look back to — a marker of time, a definitive place showing that I Did The Thing.

So, yeah. I'm going to try and slow down, take a breath, and collect some memories. Feel free to join in for the ride.
